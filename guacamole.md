# Guacamole Recipe
## Ingredients
- avocado
- cilantro
- salt
- pepper
- chile
- garlic
- lime
- onion
- hot pepper
  
## Instructions
1. Mash the avocado with joy or agression, you decide.
2. Chop the onion, hot-pepper, cilantro and garlic finely
3. Add salt & pepper 
4. Squeeze the onion and mix all up
5. Enjoy it!
